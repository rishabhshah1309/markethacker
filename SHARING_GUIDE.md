3. **Push to GitHub**

4. **Deploy to Streamlit Cloud**
- Go to [share.streamlit.io](https://share.streamlit.io)

- Click "Deploy"

**🎉 Your app will be live at: `https://YOUR_APP_NAME.streamlit.app`**
